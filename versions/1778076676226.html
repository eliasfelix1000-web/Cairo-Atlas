Metricool API lags ~24 hours, so when the date range is Today, the Organic content section automatically falls back to yesterday and labels it. Channel buckets follow Sidekick conventions: Meta paid is utm_source ∈ (meta/fb/ig) with paid medium; Instagram/TikTok/Facebook organic require utm_medium = social_organic; Klaviyo email matches referrer_source = email; Google organic (SEO) matches referrer_source = search. Direct = no UTM, no qualifying referrer. Other = anything that doesn't match. Dates in shop timezone (Europe/Stockholm).
