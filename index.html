Sources: Shopify run-analytics-query — sessions (utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, referrer_source, referrer_name) and sales (order_utm_*, referring_channel). Organic content from Metricool — get_instagram_reels + get_tiktok_videos on blog 6200400. Metricool API lags ~24 hours, so when the date range is Today, the Organic content section automatically falls back to yesterday and labels it. Channel buckets: Meta paid is utm_source in the IG/FB/Meta family with a paid medium. Organic platforms keep variant granularity — utm_source=IgStory shows as "Instagram organic — Story", igreel as "— Reel", igbio as "— Bio", plain ig/instagram stays as "Instagram organic". Same for tt* → TikTok and fb* → Facebook. The Content → Site funnel rolls all variants of a platform back up so per-platform totals stay correct. Klaviyo email matches referrer_source = email; Google organic (SEO) matches referrer_source = search. Direct = no UTM, no qualifying referrer. Other = anything that doesn't match. Dates in shop timezone (Europe/Stockholm). Shopify analytics lags 5–15 minutes — current-day numbers settle as hours close.
